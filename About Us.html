About Us

Mission Statement

Our goal is our commitment to providing the knowledge and skills required to prepare students to innovate and develop relevant technologies.

CHIPS Week

Every year, the club is granted three days in November for 'CHIPS Week', where the club hosts various activities. These include competitions and seminars (especially the competitions, which most are excited to see).

Tutoring

CHIPS provides tutoring sessions for those who need them, such Data Structures and Programming. Meetings are usually held each week, and you can request a topic to study.

Exploring Opportunities

Through hackathons or job opportunities, CHIPS is ideal for finding technology-based events that members can engage in. You can find events taking place both on and off the campus.
