The CHIPS Club

The Computer Hardware Information Processing Specialists (CHIPS) club exists to promote the spirit of enquiry and dedication and to keep students in touch with the frontiers of Computer Science, while supporting their creative interest and abilities in technological skills.

Description:

CHIPS, Computer Hardware Information Processing Specialists, is the academic club of the Department of Computer and Information Sciences, Northern Caribbean University. It has a membership that exceeds three hundred students that major or minor in a programme in the department.
